The Southern Colonies are Maryland, Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, and Georgia. This region features broad rivers and vast wetlands that gradually merged with the sea. The soil and the hot and wet cimate were ideal for farming. The king appointed a governor, but otherwise the colonies were democratic. Because the colony is populated by enlish people it is mostly christian (most of england was christian at the time).
The Middle Colonies are New York, Pennsylvania, New Jersey, Deleware. They have rich soil and wooded mountains, perfect for farming and lumbering. The government was democratic along with a governer appointed by the king of England. The people who settled the middle colonies represented many cultures and religions, so they are very religously tolerant.
The New England Colonies are New Hampshire, Massachusetts, Rhode Island, and Conneticut. The region has rocky hilly wilderness, theres also forests and sea. They made their economy on lumbering, fishing, shipbuilding, and trade. These colonies were self governed with a governer appointed by the king of England. The first settlers came to avoid religous prosecution, Massachusetts organized a theocracy before a democracy.
